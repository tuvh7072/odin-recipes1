My taco recipe

Ingredients

  • Taco powder
  • Sweet corn
  • Pepper
  • Cucumber
  • Tomatoes
  • Guacamole
  • Pico de gallo
  • Tofu
  • Wraps

Steps:

  1. Scramble the tofu and fry it in oil.
  2. Once the tofu is starting to get colour, add taco powder and water
  3. Cut up the vegetables and add them to the table
  4. Assemble your wrap to your liking

Fried rice

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Peas
  • Carrot
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Egg
  • Sauces:
    • Soy sauce
    • Hoisin
    • Sesame oil

Steps:

  1. Dice carrots, onion, and garlic.
  2. Fry carrots.
  3. Add rice, onion, garlic,and peas.
  4. Crack the egg, stir, and then add the sauces.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
